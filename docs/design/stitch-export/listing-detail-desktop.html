The Galle Fort Manor

LKR 45,000 / night

Nestled within the UNESCO World Heritage site of the Galle Fort, The Galle Fort Manor is a meticulously restored 18th-century merchant’s home. This boutique sanctuary offers a rare intersection of Dutch colonial history and contemporary tropical luxury.

Every corner of the manor tells a story, from the hand-carved ebony furniture to the original limestone walls that have stood for nearly three hundred years. Guests can enjoy panoramic views of the Indian Ocean from the rooftop terrace or find respite in the central verdant courtyard.

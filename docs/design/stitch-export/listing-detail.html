The Galle Fort Manor

STARTING AT
LKR 45,000 / NIGHT

Nestled within the historic walls of the UNESCO World Heritage site, The Galle Fort Manor is a meticulously restored 18th-century merchant’s villa that bridges the gap between colonial grandeur and modern tropical minimalism. Every corner of this estate speaks to a time when life moved with the rhythm of the tides and the monsoon winds.

Featuring high ceilings, original Dutch-period masonry, and a sprawling inner courtyard that serves as a sanctuary from the bustling fort streets, the manor offers six bespoke suites. Each space is curated with local antiques and custom-designed furniture that respects the architectural heritage of the island while providing the comfort expected by the global modern traveler.

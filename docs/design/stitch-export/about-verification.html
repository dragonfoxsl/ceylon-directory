Our Commitment

A hand-checked directory for the discerning traveler.

In an era of automated scrapers and unverified listings, we return to the artisanal approach. Every boutique hotel, heritage villa, and curated experience in Ceylon Directory has been personally visited and verified by our team of curators.

The Verification Standard

STEP 01

Submit

Partners submit their service details through our rigorous application portal. We require full transparency regarding heritage status, environmental impact, and community engagement.

STEP 02

Manual Review

Our curators conduct an unannounced site visit. We don't just look at the amenities; we measure the soul of the experience—from the quality of the linen to the authenticity of the local interaction.

STEP 03

Live

Only 15% of applicants make it to the final directory. Once live, the listing becomes a part of our heritage ecosystem, promoted to a global audience that values quality over quantity.
